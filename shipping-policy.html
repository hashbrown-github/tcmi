Shipping

Shipping costs are not included for domestic or international orders and will be charged separately

Shipping charges for your orders will be calculated and displayed at checkout.

Shipping charges vary depending on the total weight of the items, the delivery address location (domestic or international), and any applicable taxes or duties.

Please note that standard delivery timelines do not include Saturdays, Sundays, and Public Holidays as working days.

Our delivery time ranges from 5 working days to 10 working days.

Please allow 3-5 business days for order processing before shipment. We will notify you by email once your order has been shipped along with tracking information.

If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days. Please allow additional days in transit for delivery. If there will be a significant delay in shipment of your order, we will contact you via email or telephone.

Tracking

After your order is shipped, we will send you an email and text containing the shipping company details and the tracking information for your order.

With this information, you can directly track your package on the shipping company's website by entering the provided tracking details.

Non-Availability on Delivery

If our delivery partners are unable to deliver the product to your address, they will return it to us. To ensure smooth delivery, kindly provide a complete and accurate shipping address. This information is crucial in expediting the delivery of your order.

In case of a non-delivery due to a mistake made by you, such as providing an incorrect name, address, or contact number, any additional costs incurred for re-delivery will be your responsibility. Therefore, it is essential to double-check and provide accurate details to avoid any inconvenience.

Damages

The Cloth Merchant of India is not liable for any products damaged or lost during shipping. If you receive your order damaged, please contact the shipment carrier to file a claim.

Please save all packaging materials and damaged goods before filing a claim.
